What Is History

The Indian subcontinent, the great landmass of South Asia, is the home of one of the world’s oldest and
most influential civilizations. In this article, the subcontinent, which for historical purposes is usu-
ally called simply “India,” is understood to comprise the areas of not only the present-day Republic of
India but also the republics of Pakistan (partitioned from India in 1947) and Bangladesh (which formed
the eastern part of Pakistan until its independence in 1971)
